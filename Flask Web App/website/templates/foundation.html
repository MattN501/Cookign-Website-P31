Anyone can cook

Anyone can cook because cooking is a skill that grows with practice, not perfection. With countless recipes, tutorials, and resources available online, even beginners can start with simple dishes and gradually build confidence in the kitchen. Cooking isn’t about mastering gourmet techniques overnight—it’s about learning to understand ingredients, flavors, and basic methods like boiling, sautéing, or baking. Mistakes are part of the process and often lead to better results the next time. Whether you’re making a quick pasta or experimenting with new spices, anyone with curiosity and a willingness to learn can become a capable home cook.
